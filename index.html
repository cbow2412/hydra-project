Our Mission 🌍

At Hydra Technologies Ltd, a Nairobi-based startup, our mission is to eradicate the daily frustration of long queues plaguing millions. Whether at Huduma Centres, Safaricom Shops, hospitals, or banks, people lose 1-5 hours daily—time better spent with family, work, or personal growth. We aim to transform service access into a seamless, inclusive experience, starting in Kenya and scaling across Africa. Our legacy? Turning wasted hours into productivity, empowered by technology and partnerships with giants like Equity Bank and Safaricom. 🌱

Our Technology Stack 🔧

Hydra leverages a hybrid IoT-Cloud architecture, integrating effortlessly with existing ticketing systems without disruption. Here’s the deep dive:

  • IoT Edge Layer 🎮: A Raspberry Pi device, costing under KSh 15,000 per site, sits on the same subnet as ticket dispensers. It "sniffs" real-time queue activity and customer database updates, requiring no staff training or system overhauls.
  • AWS Cloud Backend ☁️: - DynamoDB: Securely stores queue and user data. - Redis: Delivers millisecond-level real-time updates. - Lambda: Orchestrates ticket injection and payment flows. - SageMaker: Analyzes patterns (e.g., peak hours) for optimization. - MQTT Broker: Ensures secure, low-latency communication.
  • Middle Layer 🛠️: The FATHER Database bridges the Pi and AWS, injecting tickets via Ethernet/API extensions found in Kenyan machines.
  • Frontend 🌐: A mobile-friendly website (https://sites.google.com/view/project-hydra/home) with future app plans, featuring live queue views and M-PESA payments (KSh 35 fee).

Security 🔒: End-to-end encryption protects data, with no institutional computer exposure—Pi communicates solely with the ticket database and cloud.
