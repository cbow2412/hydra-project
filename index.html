Our Mission 🌍

At Hydra Technologies Ltd, a Nairobi-based startup, our mission is to eradicate the daily frustration of long queues ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐. Whether at Huduma Centres, Safaricom Shops, hospitals, or banks, people lose 1-5 hours daily. We aim to transform service access into a seamless, inclusive experience, starting in Kenya and scaling across Africa.

Our Technology Stack 🔧

  • IoT Edge Layer 🎮: Raspberry Pi devices "sniff" queue activity, requiring no staff training.
  • AWS Cloud Backend ☁️: DynamoDB, Redis, Lambda, SageMaker, MQTT Broker for real-time, scalable processing.
  • Frontend 🌐: Mobile-friendly website with live queue updates & M-PESA integration.

Our Goals 🎯

Short-Term 🚀: Pilot at Kahawa Sukari Kianjokoma Plaza with 10,000 daily users. Long-Term 🌍: Scale to 30,000+ daily users across Kenya and Africa.

Simplicity at Its Core 🌱

Hydra is built for ease: check queue, book, arrive. Inclusive, real-time, clean interface that saves hours for users ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐.

Advantages ✅

  • Time Savings ⏳: 80% reduction per user
  • Revenue 💸: KSh 35 fee per booking
  • Data Insights 📊: Track 30k+ daily visits
  • Inclusivity 🤝: No disruption, secure for all
  • Scalability 🌐: Plug-and-play for 1,000+ institutions
